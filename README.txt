Game introduction:

Little green blob tries to stay alive by avoiding the blue blobs and gets points for every second he stays alive. He can get extra points by hovering over the rat power up repeatedly.


To play the game:

Click start game when ready to play. Move the character up, left, down, and right with the WASD buttons respectively to dodge the blue blobs. Hover over the rat to get 10 extra points each time.
